Research & Innovation Ecosystem

JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) has made sincere efforts to address societal issues through cutting-edge research and innovation. The University encourages researchers and faculty to undertake quality research through independent and collaborative initiatives with a multi and trans-disciplinary approach, fostering an environment that results in significant intellectual property.

Research Vision

Our goal is to generate new knowledge that fosters sustainable development through collaborative research with emphasis on multi-disciplinary approaches that address real-world challenges.

4.3 Short-term Goals

  • Encourage graduate students in research processes
  • Enhance infrastructural facilities (libraries, labs)
  • Promote interdisciplinary research projects
  • Support innovative research in thrust areas
  • Conduct workshops on research methodology
  • Emphasize research ethics and integrity
  • Organize orientation for research guides
  • Encourage collaborative research programs
  • Provide fellowships to attract research scholars

4.4 Medium-term Goals

  • Increase number of Research Centers
  • Train faculty to become effective research guides
  • Provide financial support for research projects
  • Focus on Sustainable Development Goals
  • Adopt AI/ML tools to enhance research quality
  • Establish research clusters for collaboration
  • Support Post-Doctoral studies

4.5 Long-term Goals

  • Collaborate with foreign universities for faculty exchange
  • Attract international PhD and post-doctoral students
  • Publish in high-impact interdisciplinary journals
  • Establish strong IPR cell for patents and copyrights
  • Invite industries to set up campus labs
  • Encourage faculty consultancy services
  • Strengthen technology-driven faculty development
  • Enhance placement through industry collaborations
